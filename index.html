Together, We Can Create a Brighter Future

Mike Foundation is a Charity organization formed in the year 2018 by Michael Sulemana who is a season Engineer and Businessman. The foundation has a strong Membership of over 50 people who are the backbone of it growth and the general public. The primary purpose of the foundation is to give back to society like Orphans home, prisons, Mental homes, hospitals, school in rural communities, health screening and education, education on climate change and Entrepreneurship on capacity building.

About Us

At mike foundation , our mission is to ensure everyone has access to basic needs and opportunities. We envision a future free from poverty and inequality through education, healthcare, and sustainable development. Founded in 2018, we began as a small initiative to support needy. Our programs have since impacted thousands of lives. We focus on key areas: education, healthcare, and sustainability. Join us in making a difference through donations, volunteering, or spreading the word. Together, we can build a brighter future for all. For more information, contact us or follow us on social media. Thank you for your support!
